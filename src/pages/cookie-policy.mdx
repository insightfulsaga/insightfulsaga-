# **Cookie Policy**

_Last updated: {new Date().toLocaleDateString()}_

This Cookie Policy explains how **Insightful Saga** uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience.

---

## **1. What Are Cookies?**

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function, remember preferences, and understand usage patterns.

---

## **2. Types of Cookies We Use**

### **2.1. Essential Cookies**
Required for basic website functionality, such as:

- Navigation  
- Page rendering  
- Security  

These cannot be disabled.

---

### **2.2. Analytics Cookies**
Used to understand how users interact with our website:

- Pages visited  
- Time on page  
- Traffic sources  
- Device and browser info  

We may use tools such as **Google Analytics**.

---

### **2.3. Functional Cookies**
Used to remember:

- User preferences  
- Theme selection  
- Settings  

---

## **3. Why We Use Cookies**

We use cookies to:

- Improve performance  
- Personalize user experience  
- Understand traffic patterns  
- Enhance site security  

---

## **4. Managing Cookies**

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

- Block cookies  
- Remove cookies  
- Allow only certain types of cookies  

Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

---

## **5. Third-Party Cookies**

We may allow trusted third-party services to set cookies:

- Analytics tools  
- Embedded content  
- Search optimization tools  

These providers follow their own policies.
