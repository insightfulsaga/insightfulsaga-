# Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often do you publish new tutorials?
A: I aim to update the site regularly as I learn new concepts — usually weekly.

### Q: Do I need prior coding experience to follow along?
A: Not at all. I start with beginner-friendly explanations and gradually move to advanced concepts.

### Q: Can I suggest a topic or tutorial?   
A: Yes! You can reach out via email if you’d like me to cover something specific.   

### Q: Is the content free?
A: Yes. All tutorials and guides are freely available.

### Q: Can I use the code examples in my own projects?
A: Absolutely — the examples are here to help you practice and build your own work.

### Q: Do you cover only PySpark and Databricks?
A: No. While I focus on PySpark, Databricks, and data platforms, I also plan to expand into other areas like Python basics, Snowflake, Azure, and more.

### Q: Will you add videos or downloadable content?
A: Yes! I plan to add multiple learning formats (PDFs, videos, notebooks) as the site grows.

### Q: How can I stay updated on new tutorials?
A: You can bookmark the site or subscribe to future updates once a newsletter option is added
